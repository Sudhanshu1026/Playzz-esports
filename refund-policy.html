Refund Policy

This Refund Policy outlines the rules regarding entry fee refunds for tournaments organized by PlayZZ.

1. Entry Fee Refunds

Once a slot is successfully booked and confirmed, entry fees are non-refundable.

2. Event Cancellation

If a tournament is canceled by PlayZZ due to unforeseen circumstances, registered players may receive a full refund or credit for future events.

3. Disqualification

No refund will be provided if a player or team is disqualified due to rule violations, misconduct, or failure to join the lobby on time.

4. Duplicate or Failed Payments

In case of duplicate payments or failed transactions, players can contact us with valid proof for resolution.

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds (if applicable) may take 5–7 working days to process.

6. Contact

For refund-related queries, please contact us through the Contact section or WhatsApp support.
